Everything You Need
to Curate Nature

Say goodbye to scattered notes and lost photos. We provide a centralized, interactive workspace where preserving, organizing, and exploring your plant data is faster and more intuitive than ever.

Comprehensive Cataloging

Easily document every detail. Add scientific names, geographic origins, images, and specific traits. Organize your findings in structured, easy-to-manage private or public workspaces.

Advanced Search

Instantly find exactly what you need using multi-criteria search—like filtering by soil type, origin, and benefits. Seamlessly export your entire database in open JSON and XML formats.

Collaborative Workspaces

Invite friends and colleagues to contribute to your herbaria. Assign strict Editor or Viewer roles to build vast collections together while keeping full control over your data.

Rooted in Passion,
Built for Discovery.

We built this platform out of a shared frustration traditional herbaria are beautiful but they are fragile hard to share and easily lost. We wanted to bridge the gap between our love for nature and modern web technologies. Our mission is simple to give students researchers and hobbyists a secure collaborative digital home where botanical knowledge can be preserved organized and shared globally without limits.
