About us

Ever since he was a child, Sebastian found more inspiration in the intricate patterns of a leaf than in a textbook. An avid hiker and nature photographer, he spent years documenting the flora of the Carpathian Mountains. However, he soon realized that physical notebooks couldn't keep up with the vast diversity he encountered. This frustration birthed the vision for HerbaLink. As the design lead, Sebastian’s mission was to create an interface that feels as organic as the plants it catalogs. For him, this project isn't just code—it’s a digital sanctuary for every explorer who believes that nature’s wonders deserve to be preserved with the same care they were discovered with.
